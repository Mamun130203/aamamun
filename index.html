About Me

I am a researcher specializing in Computer Vision and Self-Supervised Learning. Currently, I am pursuing my PhD at the School of Information and Communication, Griffith University, and working with CSIRO to solve complex agricultural problems using AI.

My current research, "Plant Disease Detection Using Self-Supervised Learning," aims to reduce the reliance on large labeled datasets in precision agriculture. Previously, I served as the Chairman of the EEE Department at Feni University.
