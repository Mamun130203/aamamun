Biography

The Researcher

I am a researcher bridging the gap between Deep Learning and Precision Agriculture. Currently pursuing my PhD at Griffith University, I am developing novel self-supervised learning algorithms to detect plant diseases with minimal labeled data.

My journey began at Feni University as an Assistant Professor & Chairman, following my Master's in Engineering Science from Multimedia University, Malaysia. I have secured multiple grants, including the FRGS Malaysia, and my work is currently applied in collaboration with CSIRO.
